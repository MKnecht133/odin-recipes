Pear Delight

A quick southern dessert, don't knock it 'til you try it!

Ingredients

  • Can of Pear Halves in syrup.
  • Mayonnaise (We prefer Duke's or Hellman's)
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese (or sharp cheese of choice)

Steps

  1. Strain pear halves and lay on serving plate.
  2. add one dollop of mayonnaise to the center "bowl" of each half.
  3. top with cheese and serve
  4. Bon appetit!
