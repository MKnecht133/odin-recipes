Easy Chicken Bake

A Chicken recipe that's "set it, and forget it."

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken breast
  • BBQ sauce of choice
  • an oven

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350*
  2. grease a baking sheet with olive or vegetable oil.
  3. Bake chicken at 350* for 30 minutes
  4. Flip chicken over and baste with favorite BBQ sauce. Bake for an additional 20m
  5. Flip one last time and baste the other side, cooking for a final 10m
  6. Serve it up! - this dish pairs great with the Southern Greens Recipe.
